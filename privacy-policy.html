Boss Block Puzzle

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Boss Block Puzzle ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you play our game. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

The account backup feature is completely optional. You can play Boss Block Puzzle without creating an account or providing any personal information.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide (optional):

  • Email address — only if you choose to create an account for cloud backup
  • Player display name — only if you choose to set one
  • Password — stored securely using Firebase Authentication (we never see your plain password)

Game data automatically saved:

  • Your game scores and personal best
  • World leaderboard entries (player name + score only)

We do NOT collect:

  • Your real name, phone number, or physical address
  • Device location or GPS data
  • Contacts, photos, or any files on your device
  • Any information from children under 13

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your scores on the world leaderboard
  • To back up your game progress across devices (if you create an account)
  • To allow you to restore your account on a new device

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Firebase and Google Services

We use Firebase (by Google) to power our backend services:

  • Firebase Authentication — handles secure account login and signup
  • Cloud Firestore — stores leaderboard scores and optional account data

Firebase's data processing is governed by Google's Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

5. Advertising

Boss Block Puzzle may display advertisements powered by Google AdMob. AdMob may use device advertising identifiers to show relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings under Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

Google AdMob's privacy policy: policies.google.com/technologies/ads

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. All data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored securely using Firebase's infrastructure. Passwords are never stored in plain text.

7. Children's Privacy

Boss Block Puzzle is a general audience game. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the app
  • Play the game without creating an account

To delete your account data, sign in to the app and use the Sign Out option, or contact us at the email below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the game after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
